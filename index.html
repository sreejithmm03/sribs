Executive Summary

During the reporting period, I actively contributed to the academic, research and institutional development of KSCSTE–Srinivasa Ramanujan Institute for Basic Sciences through original research, academic outreach, conference presentations, invited lectures, programme organisation and institutional documentation. My research focused on modular forms, Siegel modular forms and Jacobi forms, leading to multiple research manuscripts, conference presentations and collaborations. Alongside research, I organised academic events, delivered invited talks, coordinated outreach programmes and contributed to the institute's documentation, website and social media activities.
